Melanie Toh's Final Project Proposal

TOPIC

My website will showcase my portfolio and act as a more detailed and visual resumé.

AUDIENCE

The audience will be recruiters, employers, and eventually people interested in my artwork when I develop a larger artwork portfolio.

DESIGN & STYLE

  1. NAVIGATION
    • Minimalistic design (not the standard bar)
    • Create my own logo to be placed either in the top left corner that directs people back to the main page
    • About me link redirection next to my logo
  2. MAIN PAGE
    • Emulate Little Thunder’s Behance site’s formatting with a basic ‘about me’ panel to the left of the screen and the artworks on the right
  3. PAST PROJECTS PAGE
    • Emulate Coolors’s rounded rectangle setup like below but for the sites I’ve made in the past
    • Hovering over it will provide a brief description and clicking on it will redirect users to the relevant section on the page
  4. CONTACT ME PAGE
    • Contact me form on the left is standard
    • Social media platforms on the right similar to Hyperbole and a Half's
  5. FOOTER
    • Standard copyright info at the bottom of the page
  6. COLOR PALETTE
    • Primary color: Off white
    • Secondary colors: Tan, dark brown
  7. INSPIRATION
    • Little Thunder's Behance Site
    • Coolors
    • Hyperbole and a Half

SCOPE

  • PAGES (4 PAGES)
    • Main page: Gallery style page that showcases my artworks and their information
    • About me page: Basic information about me laid out in a resume-type format but more informal
      • Includes links to social media and a 'contact me' form
    • Past projects page: Non-artwork creations, such as past websites I've made or coding projects, will be showcased here
      • Each section is a separate project
      • Documents the creation process and provide appropriate links
    • Contact me page: Contact me form on the left and social media platforms on the right
